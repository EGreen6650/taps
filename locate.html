You might be wondering: Why does this information matter?

Knowing the correct places to recycle your used items can help our Earth become not only a better place but healthy and more environmentally friendly. You can know how to dispose of products that you might not have thought could be recyclable correctly, via our data table.

Recycling can do many beneficial things such as:

  • Save energy
  • Reduce substantial air and water pollution
  • Lessen the need for processing raw materials
  • Scale down greenhouse gas emissions that could help save our Earth from the negative effects of climate change
